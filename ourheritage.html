Our Heritage

Our Canadian Pride

Located in the heart of the Cypress National park among towering firs and ancient cedars. The gym is nestled on the crest of a hill offering stunning 360 degree views of the valley beneath it. In order to preserve and promote Canada's natural beauty we donate a portion of our profits to conservation agencies dedicating to protecting our wilderness.

Our Inspiration

Gold's Gym, Muscle Beach, and Vince's Gym. We hope to honour not only these gyms but also the greats who trained there. Our aim is to inspire a new generation with the hard work grit and determination housed in those hallowed halls. We hope to revitalize the spirits of Schwartzeneggar, Coleman, and Platz.
